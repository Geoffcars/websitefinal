Welcome to Cornerstone Charity

Empowering bright underprivileged children in Kenya through food, shelter, and education.

Our Vision

To create a world where every child has access to food, shelter, and quality education.

Our Mission

To support underprivileged but talented children in Kenya by providing essential services and opportunities.

About Us

Cornerstone Charity is dedicated to building a brighter future by meeting the basic and educational needs of children in Kenya. We believe in sustainable and impactful change through community support.
